Descent Profile is a simple descent planning tool designed for aircraft without VNAV. Enter the altitude you need to lose, your current groundspeed, and remaining distance ‚Äî the app instantly gives you the time available and the required vertical speed to make the descent.

Key Features

  • Instant required vertical speed (fpm) calculation
  • Time-to-go based on distance and groundspeed
  • Designed for fast in-flight and pre-descent planning
  • Clean, distraction-free interface
  • No accounts, no tracking

How to Use

Descent planning is simpler when you know two things: how much altitude you need to lose and how much time you have. Time comes from distance and groundspeed.

Descent Profile gives you the vertical speed required to meet your altitude constraint.

1) Set the Descent

  1. Set your CRZ LVL (cruise flight level/altitude).
  2. Set your TO LVL (target level/altitude).
  3. The app computes DESCEND (altitude to lose).

2) Enter Distance and Groundspeed

  1. Enter remaining DIST (NM) to your restriction/point.
  2. Enter GS (knots).

3) Read the Result

  1. The app shows TIME available to cover the distance.
  2. The app shows required V/S in fpm to descend the needed altitude within that time.
Tip: Use it as a quick cross-check for your mental math or SOP descent planning.
